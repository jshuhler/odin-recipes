Chicken Noodle Soup

It's a bowl of chicken and noodles. In soup!

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Chicken broth
  • Vegetable broth
  • Cooked chicken bits
  • Noods
  • A bunch of different herbs

Method

  1. Heat up those veggies
  2. Honestly, just put everything else together in a pot.
  3. Cook up them noodles in the liquid with everything else.
  4. When they're done, it's done. Don't over think this.
