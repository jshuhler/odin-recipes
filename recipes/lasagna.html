Lasagna

It's a whole mess of pasta, sauce, meat, and cheese. Then it's all those things again. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Minced onion
  • Garlic, crushed
  • Some tomatoes, crushed
  • Some more tomatoes, sauced
  • A bunch of different herbs
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • An unhatched chicken

Method

  1. Cook. That. Meat.
  2. Add your tomatoes, some herbs, then cook it slowly for a long time.
  3. Boil those weird noodle sheets.
  4. Mix the cheese, some herbs left on the counter, and the unhatched chicken.
  5. Layers!
  6. Bake it covered for 25 minutes, then uncover for another 25 minutes. Do it as hot as you want, I don't care.
  7. You're going to burn your tongue, let it cool for 15 minutes.
