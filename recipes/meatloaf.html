Meatloaf

It's like a loaf of bread but made out of meat.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Minced onion
  • Garlic, crushed
  • Bread crumbs
  • Some tomatoes, sauced
  • A bunch of different herbs
  • Catsup
  • Worchechsershire Sauce
  • Some other things
  • An unhatched chicken, beaten

Method

  1. Cook up that onion and garlic.
  2. Basically mix everything else in a big bowl.
  3. Put it in a loaf pan.
  4. Make it hot.
  5. Mix Catsup and Tomato sauce together, cover loaf. Keep making it hot.
  6. Eat the loaf of meat you made. Use it as a replacement for sandwich bread for a BLT, you weirdo.
